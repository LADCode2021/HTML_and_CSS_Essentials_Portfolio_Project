About First Photography

Welcome to First Photography. We work closely with clients to ensure they have lasting memories of new additions to their families with a professional look and feel. We have experience shooting cake smashes, newborn shoots, religious and naming ceremonies, family portraits and many more special moments.

We are based in the South West of England but are happy to travel for the right projects!

About John Smith

My name is John Smith and I am an experienced photographer specialising in capturing those first special moments with your new family member! My passion comes from having children of my own and being forever grateful that my families first moments were captured.
